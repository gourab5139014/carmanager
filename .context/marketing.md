# Marketing & Growth Brain

## Positioning
**"The Frictionless Fuel Tracker."**
Positioning against Drivvo/Fuelly by focusing on speed and zero manual entry.

## Target Audience
- **Car Enthusiasts:** People who track MPG for fun or performance.
- **Gig Drivers:** Dashers, Uber drivers who need precise expense tracking.
- **Utility Users:** People who just want to know when they last changed their oil.

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Technical but accessible. Utilitarian. Fast. 
- **Voice:** "We value your time."
- **Style:** Monochrome, dark-mode, minimal gradients, emphasis on data visualizations.

## Key Features (The "Hooks")
1. **Photo-First:** Snap a photo of your odometer, and the app does the rest.
2. **Privacy-Native:** Your data stays in your Supabase project. No third-party ad tracking.
3. **Always Edge:** Fast responses globally via Edge Functions.

## SEO Keywords
- Mileage tracker
- Gas receipt OCR
- Supabase car manager
- MPG dashboard
- Fuel economy calculator
